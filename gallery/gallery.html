What is low-waste baking?

My efforts to stay low-waste include:
  • using the whole of quantized ingredients
  • refactoring recipes to be small-scale
  • freezing and saving leftover dough
  • not discarding dough edges or leftovers
  • minimizing non-recyclable waste
